      Old King's Road

      #location

      Connected Locations: Saltmarsh, Bent Willow Tavern, Feywild leak

      Ancient road running along the storm-shattered ward-stone line, now experiencing reality distortions.

      Current State

      • Storm damage has created cracks in the magical ward-stones
      • Reality misbehaves along the route - weather anomalies and strange encounters
      • Checkpoint scenes with petty officials noting unusual Feywild/Witchlight traffic
      • Road leads directly to Feywild leak area

      Key Locations Along Route

      • Saltmarsh: Starting point for the party's journey
      • Bent Willow Tavern: Major checkpoint and hub for Scarlet Brotherhood operations
      • Feywild Leak: Destination where reality tears into the Feywild

      Travel Conditions

      • Use weather tables and random encounter tables to show reality glitching
      • Storm-scarred landscape with visible ward-stone damage
      • Increased Feywild creature sightings and magical anomalies
      • Tihildur's storm powers may resonate with the damaged ward-stones

      Historical Significance

      • Ancient protective ward-stones maintained barrier between planes
      • Scarlet Brotherhood, Cyrieth Nerolien, and other mortals were manipulating ward-stones before storm hit
      • Tihildur's storm event was same magical disturbance that cracked the ward-line
      • Now serves as pathway to investigate planar breach and its causes

      Story Importance

      • Transition zone from Saltmarsh political intrigue to Feywild weirdness
      • Demonstrates connection between Tihildur's powers and world-changing events
      • Route to confront both mortal culprits (Cyrieth Nerolien/Scarlet Brotherhood) and planar threats
