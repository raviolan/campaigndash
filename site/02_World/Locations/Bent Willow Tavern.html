      Bent Willow Tavern

      #location #tavern

      Location: Old King's Road

      NPCs: Lysa "Ledger" Feld, Brother Calem, Garrik, Ellywick, Diana

      Major tavern and hub along the storm-damaged ward-stone line. Serves as checkpoint between Saltmarsh and the Feywild leak.

      Atmosphere

      • Goblin wrestling and carnival energy
      • Glitching mirror as visible sign of Feywild leak
      • Rowdy but welcoming to travelers
      • Party caused chaos with betting during goblin wrestling match
      • Drink named "Valkrath Goblin Tosser" (green chartreuse with olive oil) after Val'krath

      The Ledger System

      • Secret exchange where patrons trade secrets
      • Tihildur left confession about his slave and domineering nature
      • Received secret: "Do NOT let musicians tune instruments facing the glass"
      • Managed by Lysa "Ledger" Feld who keeps second set of books for Scarlet Brotherhood/Cyrieth Nerolien jobs

      Key NPCs

      • Lysa "Ledger" Feld - nervous half-elf barmaid/bookkeeper with Scarlet Brotherhood connections
      • Brother Calem - mysterious figure with Scarlet Brotherhood ties
      • Garrik - local with hidden knowledge
      • Ellywick - musician with magical lute strings that whisper secrets
      • Diana - centaur cursed by Skabatha Nightshade, foreshadowing Feywild dangers

      Strategic Importance

      • Safe-but-shifty hub for ongoing operations
      • Scarlet Brotherhood/Cyrieth Nerolien connection point
      • Gateway area to Feywild investigations
      • Reality glitches and Feywild bleed-through events
