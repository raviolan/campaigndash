The Storm

Recently, Saltmarsh are being hit by a wrong-feeling storm. Lightning in strange colors, thunder like distant voices. Its almost as if the storm is looking for something.

DM Truth

  • The Feywild leak is not a Feywild plan. It is a scar.
  • The world/nature, strained by mortal meddling with old ward-stones and deep magic, unleashed a once-in-a-century storm.
  • That storm chose Tihildur as its lightning rod, awakening his storm sorcerer powers.
  • The space-time plane stuff is already thin because of the Aurumvorax summoning
  • The nearest plane "under pressure" was the Feywild, so its energy now seeps through the crack.
  • Hags/Fey are opportunists, not the original cause.
