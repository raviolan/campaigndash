Overview

#npc

Relationships: Odo Kneecapper, Tihildur, Gellan Primewater

Mid-level player in Saltmarsh's smuggling world. Works with Salabaster Shipping through intermediaries, ensuring plausible deniability for Daddy Salabaster.

Background

  • Lost one leg to Odo Kneecapper in a confrontation
  • Operates smuggling network ranging from mundane contraband to black-market alchemical supplies and magical artifacts
  • Connected to Scarlet Brotherhood operations
  • High-value relationship with Gellan Primewater

Motivations

  • Respects Daddy Salabaster's power but doesn't fear him enough to stay loyal forever
  • Opportunistic - could work with other factions like Scarlet Brotherhood to hedge his bets
  • Sees Tihildur as a potential threat to his operations

Knowledge

  • Understands exactly how he fits into the larger web of Salabaster Shipping operations
  • His parting words to Tihildur were full of implications and warnings
