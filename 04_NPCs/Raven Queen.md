#deity #shadowfell
Related: [[Tenebris]]

Goddess of death, fate, and winter. Rules over the [[Shadowfell]] and governs the transition between life and death.

## Domain and Influence
- **Realm**: [[Shadowfell]] - dark reflection of the Material Plane
- **Portfolio**: Death, fate, winter, memories of the dead
- **Followers**: Ravens, shadar-kai, those who hunt undead

## Relationship with Tenebris
- [[Tenebris]] escaped from her service in the [[Shadowfell]]
- Now exists as rogue agent on Material Plane
- **Crow Omens**: Ravens serve as her eyes and agents
- [[Tenebris]] shoots any crows on sight due to paranoia
- Collects raven feathers for arrows as defiance/protection

## Motivations Regarding Tenebris
- Views escaped servants as property to be reclaimed
- May send agents or omens to track [[Tenebris]]
- Could offer bargains or threats to other party members
- Might be willing to negotiate if offered suitable exchange

## Campaign Implications
- Source of tension and paranoia for [[Tenebris]]
- Potential supernatural threat during travel
- Could intersect with [[Feywild]] themes of otherworldly patrons
- Might have interest in [[Shadowfell]] connections to planar breaches
- Could be leveraged for information about planar boundaries and ward-stones
