Relationships: [[Tihildur]], [[Sanbalet]], [[Cyrieth Nerolien]], [[Gellan Primewater]]

Cold, calculating businessman who runs [[Salabaster Shipping]] - both its legitimate operations and shadowy dealings.

## Personality
- Pragmatic businessman who doesn't care who he works with as long as it's profitable
- Runs legitimate shipping empire on the surface while turning blind eye to smuggling operations
- Has complicated feelings about [[Tihildur]]'s meddling in family business

## Business Operations
- Primary relationship with [[Sanbalet]] through intermediaries for plausible deniability
- Smuggling goods range from mundane contraband to dangerous black-market items
- [[Gellan Primewater]] is high-value client for both legal trade and smuggling benefits
- Brother to [[Cyrieth Nerolien]] - formerly allies, now estranged due to Cyrieth's overt criminal activities

## Reaction to Tihildur's Actions
- Angry about interference in delicate smuggling operations
- Sees opportunity to mold [[Tihildur]] and bring him into the fold
- May issue ultimatum: work for him officially or be cut off from family resources
- Considers arranging political marriage to tie [[Tihildur]] deeper into family affairs
