#faction #organization 
Related NPCs: [[Cyrieth Nerolien]], [[Skerrin Wavechaser]], [[Anders Solmor]]
Locations: [[Bent Willow Tavern]], [[Saltmarsh]]

Secretive faction seeking to restore Suloise noble houses to power and destabilize the region.

## Goals
- Restore Suloise noble houses to political dominance
- Destabilize [[Keoland]] and [[Saltmarsh]] through manipulation and infiltration
- Oppose [[Radiant Citadel]] influence and foreign settlers
- Install Brotherhood agents in positions of power

## Operations in Saltmarsh Region
- **Primary Agent**: [[Skerrin Wavechaser]] - manipulates [[Anders Solmor]] and [[Saltmarsh Council]]
- **Fixer/Broker**: [[Cyrieth Nerolien]] - operates black market connections
- **Meeting Place**: [[Bent Willow Tavern]] - [[Cyrieth Nerolien]]'s people meet here once per tenday
- **Financial**: Use cursed magical items and smuggling to fund operations

## Methods
- Manipulation through intermediaries rather than direct action
- Exploit existing tensions (lizardfolk vs humans, traditional vs loyalist)
- Spread propaganda against dwarves, lizardfolk, and [[Radiant Citadel]] foreigners
- Target [[Tihildur]]'s family due to marriage/business undermining Suloise nobility

## Key Relationships
- [[Cyrieth Nerolien]]: Uses Brotherhood resources for personal schemes
- [[Captain Xendros]]: Sometimes protects Brotherhood operatives for payment
- [[Salabaster Shipping]]: Potential target due to economic competition
- [[Tihildur]]: Threat due to family's non-Suloise business practices

## Feywild Connections
- Involved in ward-stone manipulation before the storm
- Trying to exploit [[Feywild]] leak for their own agenda
- [[Cyrieth Nerolien]]'s trail leads toward the planar breach
