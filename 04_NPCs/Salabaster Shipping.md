#organization #faction
Related NPCs: [[Daddy Salabaster]], [[Tihildur]], [[Sanbalet]], [[Gellan Primewater]]
Locations: [[Saltmarsh]]

Major shipping company with both legitimate operations and extensive smuggling network.

## Business Structure
- **Owner**: [[Daddy Salabaster]] - cold, calculating businessman
- **Heir**: [[Tihildur]] - largely unaware of smuggling operations
- **Estranged Partner**: [[Cyrieth Nerolien]] - former ally, now distanced due to overt criminality

## Operations
- **Legitimate**: Major shipping empire covering coastal trade routes
- **Illegitimate**: Smuggling network using intermediaries like [[Sanbalet]]
- **Goods**: Range from mundane contraband to black-market alchemical supplies and magical artifacts

## Key Relationships
- **[[Sanbalet]]**: Mid-level smuggling operator with plausible deniability
- **[[Gellan Primewater]]**: High-value client for both legal and illegal services
- **[[Scarlet Brotherhood]]**: Potential target due to business practices undermining Suloise nobility

## Political Position
- Essential to [[Saltmarsh]]'s economy
- Marriage and business alliances undermine traditional Suloise noble houses
- Creates tension with [[Scarlet Brotherhood]] agenda

## Family Dynamics
- [[Daddy Salabaster]] wants to bring [[Tihildur]] into full knowledge of operations
- [[Tihildur]]'s recent interference in [[Sanbalet]] operations created complications
- Risk of exposure could damage relationships with clients like [[Gellan Primewater]]
- Considering ultimatum for [[Tihildur]]: join fully or be cut off from family resources
