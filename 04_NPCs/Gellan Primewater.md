#npc #council
Relationships: [[Odo Kneecapper]], [[Sanbalet]], [[Daddy Salabaster]], [[Saltmarsh Council]]

Wealthy merchant and [[Saltmarsh Council]] member with both legitimate and illicit business interests.

## Council Position
- Traditionalist faction member
- Advocates for maintaining [[Saltmarsh]]'s fishing trade
- Opposes outside influence and crown interference
- Suspicious of dwarven mining efforts

## Business Connections
- High-value client for both [[Sanbalet]] and [[Salabaster Shipping]]
- Benefits from smuggling network while maintaining legitimate trade facade
- Disrupting [[Sanbalet]]'s operation would jeopardize his relationship with [[Daddy Salabaster]]

## Relationship with Party
- Owes [[Odo Kneecapper]] a significant favor for defending his warehouses from pirate raid
- [[Odo Kneecapper]] raised alarm and led defense, saving Gellan's business
- This debt could be leveraged by the party for information or assistance

## Personality
- Important merchant with significant wealth and influence
- Pragmatic about business relationships - willing to work with smugglers if profitable
- Protective of his commercial interests in [[Saltmarsh]]
- Balances public respectability with private profit
