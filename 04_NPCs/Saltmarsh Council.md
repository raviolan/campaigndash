### Political Factions in Saltmarsh  
These NPCs align themselves with one of the three main political factions:  

1. Traditionalists: Focused on maintaining Saltmarsh’s independence and fishing industry. Oppose the crown’s mining expansion and royal interference.
2. Loyalists: Support the crown’s efforts to turn Saltmarsh into a major trade hub and enforce law and order.
3. Scarlet Brotherhood: A shadowy faction seeking to destabilize the region and manipulate events for their own gain.
## Eda Oweland (Traditionalist)  
- Role: Senior member of the council and leader of the Traditionalist faction.
- Politics: Advocates for maintaining Saltmarsh’s fishing trade and resisting outside influence. Suspicious of the dwarves’ mining efforts and the crown’s involvement.
- Personality: Gruff, pragmatic, and fiercely protective of Saltmarsh’s fishing industry.

## Gellan Primewater (Traditionalist)  
- Role: Wealthy merchant and council member.
- Politics: Supports smuggling operations and opposes strict royal oversight, using his influence to protect the Traditionalist agenda.
- Personality: Charming and sophisticated, but secretly driven by greed and criminal activity.

## Anders Solmor (Scarlet Brotherhood Influence)  
- Role: Youngest council member, head of his family’s fishing fleet.
- Politics: Publicly opposes smuggling and the Sea Princes' slavery practices. Unknowingly manipulated by the Scarlet Brotherhood.
- Personality: Sunny and optimistic but naive and easily influenced.

## Eliander Fireborn (Loyalist)  
- Role: Captain of the Saltmarsh guard and council member.
- Politics: Represents the Loyalist faction, supporting the crown’s agenda to secure Saltmarsh as a trade hub and suppress smuggling.
- Personality: Cautious and disciplined, with a strong sense of duty to the crown.

## Manistrad Copperlocks (Loyalist)  
- Role: Leader of the dwarven mining operation and council member by royal decree.
- Politics: Prioritizes the success of the mining operation and aligns closely with the Loyalist faction’s goals.
- Personality: Curt, fair, and focused on efficiency, with little patience for dissent.

## Skerrin Wavechaser (Scarlet Brotherhood)  
- Role: Butler and secret Scarlet Brotherhood agent.
- Politics: Acts as the Brotherhood’s primary operative, manipulating Anders Solmor and working to destabilize Saltmarsh for the Brotherhood’s benefit.
- Personality: Calculating, patient, and ruthless.

## Captain Xendros (Faithful Quartermasters of Iuz)
- Role: Merchant and tiefling priest representing Iuz.
- Politics: Operates independently from the council but engages in trade deals that subtly influence local politics. Her magical item trade and interest in rare artifacts give her sway over some council members.
- Personality: Mysterious and business-savvy, with a focus on achieving her goals through cunning deals.

## Ingo the Drover  
- Role: Mercenary leader and former general hiding in Saltmarsh.
- Politics: Avoids direct involvement in politics but has connections to Eliander Fireborn. Could be drawn into political intrigue if his military expertise becomes valuable.
- Personality: Reserved and strategic, with a keen sense of survival.

## Wellgar Brinehanded (Cleric of Procan)  
- Role: Spiritual leader in Saltmarsh.
- Politics: Though not directly involved in the council, his influence over the town’s religious community gives him informal power. Supports peace and stability, aligning loosely with the Loyalists.
- Personality: Wise and calm, focused on serving the community and maintaining Saltmarsh’s connection to the sea.
