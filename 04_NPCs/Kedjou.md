Relationships: [[Sahuagin]], [[Aurumvorax]], [[Dawn Incarnate]], [[Kirana]]

Exiled wizard and corrupt priest (neutral evil) obsessed with returning "deep-earth beings" to the surface.

## Official Position
- Arcane Integration Advisor to [[Guildhall of Earth and Flame]]
- Conjuration specialist brought in by [[Radiant Citadel]] interests
- Explores magical applications for mining efficiency

## Secret Operations  
- Secretly works with [[Kirana]] to destabilize [[Copperlocks]]' hold on [[Goldwarren]]
- Allied with [[Sahuagin]] to create nightmare version of [[Aurumvorax]]
- Wants bigger influence and stronger connections to the Capital ([[Radiant Citadel]])

## The Aurumvorax Creation
- Created corrupted [[Aurumvorax]] magic on behalf of [[Sahuagin]]
- Helped transform peaceful [[Dawn Incarnate]] guardian into monstrous propaganda tool
- Used this as testbed for his own dark ambitions while serving [[Sahuagin]] agenda

## Personality
- Manipulative and calculating
- Considers most non-mages beneath him
- Opportunistic - uses [[Sahuagin]] alliance to advance hidden schemes
- Research focuses on summoning and binding extraplanar creatures
