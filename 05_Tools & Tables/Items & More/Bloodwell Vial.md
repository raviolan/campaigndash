## Official D&D 5e Item (Tasha's Cauldron of Everything)
**Bloodwell Vial**
*Wondrous item, uncommon (+1), rare (+2), or very rare (+3) (requires attunement by a sorcerer)*

**Attunement Process**: To attune to this vial, you must place a few drops of your blood into it. The vial can't be opened while your attunement to it lasts. If your attunement ends, the contained blood turns to ash.

**Spellcasting Focus & Bonus**: You can use the vial as a spellcasting focus for your spells while wearing or holding it, and you gain a bonus to spell attack rolls and to the saving throw DCs of your sorcerer spells:
- **+1 Bloodwell Vial** (Uncommon): +1 bonus
- **+2 Bloodwell Vial** (Rare): +2 bonus  
- **+3 Bloodwell Vial** (Very Rare): +3 bonus

**Sorcery Point Recovery**: When you roll any Hit Dice to recover hit points while carrying the vial, you can regain 5 sorcery points. This property can't be used again until the next dawn.
