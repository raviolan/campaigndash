Random Potions(1d20)  

1. **Quickheart Syrup**
   Gain 1d4 temporary hit points and a sugar high. You're jittery for 10 minutes.  
2. **1 Greengleam No. 7**
   You turn bright green for 24 hours. Stealth checks are at disadvantage in nature.  
3. **Driftfloat** 
   You float 1 inch above the ground for an hour. 
4. **Grumbletone Draft** 
   Your voice becomes incredibly deep and echoing for 1d4 hours. Intimidation +2.
5. **Webbybrew**
   Your hands are webbed for 1 hour. Swimming speed +10 ft 
6. **Murky brew**
   You gain darkvision 30 ft for 10 minutes. Even if you already had it, it’s now in color.
7. **Cloakfizz**
   You believe you’re invisible for 1 minute. You are not.
8. **Glimmersnort** 
   You sneeze glitter with every sentence for the next hour.
9. **Memory Fog** 
   You regain one 1st-level spell slot, or feel like you just took a nap. 
10. **Rhymevine Elixir** 
    You speak only in rhyme for the next 5 minutes.
11. **Kelpwhisk Tonic**
    Your hair grows 10 inches instantly, or if bald, you sprout seaweed locks. 
12. **Liecatcher’s Draught**
    Your tongue turns blue and you can taste lies for 10 minutes. Insight advantage.   
13. **Bubblemouth**
    You hiccup bubbles. Breathing underwater for 5 minutes is now possible.
14. **GLOWDROP ESSENCE** 
    You glow softly in the dark for 1 hour.
15. **Whispersheen**
    A small illusion of a jellyfish follows you for 24 hours. It does nothing.
16. **Doubtbrew** 
    You are convinced someone nearby is an imposter for 1 minute (WIS save ends early).
17. **Musk of the Moon-Tamer** 
    You smell amazing to beasts. Advantage on Animal Handling for 1 hour. 
18. **Bottled Thunderbelch**
    You burp once. The sound casts Thaumaturgy.
19. **Brine-Tongue Brew**
    You can speak one random underwater language for 10 minutes (Aquan, Sahuagin, etc.)  
20. **Recall of the Razor Mind**
    For 1 minute, you have perfect recall of anything you’ve ever read.
