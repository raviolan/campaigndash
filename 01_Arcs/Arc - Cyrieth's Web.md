Central Characters: [[Cyrieth Nerolien]], [[Tihildur]], [[Val'krath]], [[Scarlet Brotherhood]]

**Status:** Mostly backstory, not yet front and centre

## Arc Overview
The revelation of [[Cyrieth Nerolien]]'s web of betrayal, manipulation, and family connections that tie together multiple party members and campaign threats.

## Key Relationships
- **[[Tihildur]]'s Uncle:** [[Cyrieth Nerolien]] is [[Tihildur]]'s estranged uncle, former partner to [[Daddy Salabaster]]
- **[[Val'krath]]'s Betrayer:** Former mentor who destroyed the [[Deathgrins]] order and sold Val'krath's companions to slavers
- **[[Scarlet Brotherhood]] Broker:** Serves as fixer and agent for Brotherhood operations in [[Saltmarsh]] region

## Current Status
- Operating along [[Old King's Road]] and [[Feywild]] leak areas
- Connected to ward-stone manipulation that enabled the reality crack
- Trail leads toward the planar breach for Act 2 confrontation
- Symbol recognized by mercenaries, building reputation

## Potential Climax Scenarios
- **Act 2 Confrontation:** Face-off at the [[Feywild]] leak's heart with [[Scarlet Brotherhood]] stakes
- **Family Revelation:** [[Tihildur]] and [[Val'krath]] discover their shared connection
- **Choice Point:** Defeat, redemption, or transformation of [[Cyrieth Nerolien]] into different threat type

## Connections to Other Arcs
- **[[Salabaster Shipping]]:** Family business ties and criminal network overlaps
- **[[Feywild]] Crisis:** Cyrieth's role in creating the reality crack through ward-stone tampering
- **Political Intrigue:** [[Scarlet Brotherhood]] agenda and [[Saltmarsh]] destabilization efforts
