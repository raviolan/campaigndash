# Rumor Organization Guide

**Related Files**: [[01_Pre-Session 01 Rumors]] | [[02_Ongoing Rumor Threads]]

## Folder Structure
This folder contains rumors and information provided to PCs at various points in the campaign.

### File Types:

**00_General Campaign Rumors.md**
- Ongoing rumors that persist across multiple sessions
- Background information that's always available
- World-building rumors not tied to specific timing

**01_Pre-Session [X] Rumors.md** 
- Specific rumors provided before each session
- Include both "Push" (urgent/immediate) and "Rumor" (background) information
- Numbered by session for easy chronological tracking

**99_Resolved Rumor Threads.md**
- Archive of rumors that have been addressed or resolved
- Track how rumors developed vs. reality
- Reference for future plot threads

## Rumor Categories:

### Push
Urgent information or situations that compel PCs to action. These create immediate pressure or opportunity.

### Rumor
Background information, gossip, or hints that may or may not be true. These build atmosphere and provide potential plot hooks.

## Usage Notes:
- Review previous session rumors before creating new ones
- Consider which rumors proved true/false and how that affects PC trust
- Use rumors to foreshadow upcoming events or provide exposition
- Track which PCs received which information for consistency
